Whole exome sequencing is sensitive, rapid and efficient for detection of PCC/PGL germline mutations. Whole exome sequencing is an efficient and sensitive method for detection of germline mutations in patients with phaeochromcytomas and paragangliomas (PMID: 24102379) These results from deep sequencing demonstrate a higher mutational detection rate than reported with conventional sequencing methodology.
