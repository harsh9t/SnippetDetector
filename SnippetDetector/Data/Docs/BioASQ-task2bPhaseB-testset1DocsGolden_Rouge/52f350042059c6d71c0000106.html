AIM OF STUDY: An analysis of incidences of selected birth defects in the Czech Republic in 2000 - 2008 period. TYPE OF STUDY: Retrospective epidemiological analysis of birth defects incidences in births and total birth defects incidences from the Czech National Birth Defects Register database. MATERIAL AND METHODS: Data on pre - and postnatally diagnosed birth defects in the Czech Republic during 1994-2007 were used.
