The aim of the study was to determine the existence of oxidative stress and its possible consequences in female patients with CFS. A strong positive correlation between oxidative stress and insulin resistance was observed in metabolic syndrome. Antioxidant treatment and life style changes are indicated for women with CFS, as well as closer observation in order to assess the degree of atherosclerosis. Low levels of antioxidants and increased oxidative stress with insulin resistance in metabolic syndrome suggests that besides therapeutic life style changes as suggested in ATP III guidelines inclusion of antioxidant vitamins, fruits and vegetable could be beneficial to ward off the consequences of metabolic syndrome.
