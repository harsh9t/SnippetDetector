Emergence of many promising interventions towards this age-related condition (e. g. , physical exercise [in particular, resistance training], testosterone, antioxidant supplementations), the need for Phase II trial designs is high. The objective of this study was to evaluate the effect of omega-3 fatty acid supplementation on the rate of muscle protein synthesis in older adults. This trial was registered at clinical trials. gov as NCT00794079. Several clinical trials with androgen replacement therapy have failed to show clinical benefit. Clinical trials are needed to find better interventions for this syndrome. The extreme paucity of clinical trials on sarcopenia in literature is mainly due to difficulties in designing studies able to isolate the aging process from its multiple and interconnected consequences. .
