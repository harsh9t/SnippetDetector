This study sought to characterize the demographics of commotio cordis globally in comparison to the U. S. experience. (PMID: 21763255) Not unexpectedly, the groups differed with baseball/softball and football predominant in the United States (55% of events) and soccer, cricket, and hockey most common internationally (47% of events). (PMID: 21763255) Commotio cordis events occurred most commonly during organized sporting events (79 [62%]), such as baseball, but 49 (38%) occurred as part of daily routine and recreational activities. (PMID: 11879111).
