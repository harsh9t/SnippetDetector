The best treatment options for eradication of Helicobacter pylori involve triple or quadruple drugs therapy with different types of antibiotics. Bismuth may be also an additional option. Proton pump inhibitors are also included in treatment. The more effective drug list includes: amoxicillin, claritromycin, metronidazole rifabutin. Also chitosan microspheres with Eudragit L100 have been tested.
